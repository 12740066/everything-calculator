Rules

  1. Skins: Each hole is worth a set monetary value (Skin Value). The player with the lowest stroke count wins the skin for that hole.
  2. Handicap Compensation: Adjusted scores are calculated based on player handicaps.
  3. Bonus and Penalty: Players can earn bonuses (e.g., birdies, eagles) or incur penalties (e.g., extra strokes).
  4. Eagle and Albatross Rates: Special rates apply for eagles and albatrosses, which affect earnings calculations.
  5. NTP (Nearest To Pin): On par-3 holes, the player nearest to the pin wins bonus skins.
  6. Long Drive: On par-5 holes, the player with the longest drive (on the fairway) wins bonus skins.
  7. Carryovers: If no player wins a hole outright, the skins carry over to the next hole.
  8. Game Progress: You can save your game progress and load it later.

Enjoy your game!
